Common Questions

Answers to What Families Ask Most

We believe in total transparency. Here are the questions we hear most often, answered honestly.

Still have questions? We're happy to walk you through anything. Contact Us →

Monthly rates vary based on level of care and room type. We offer transparent, all-inclusive pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us directly for current rates — we're happy to walk you through exactly what's included and work with your family's budget.

We accept private pay and long-term care insurance. We do not currently accept Medicaid waiver programs, but our team can help connect families with financial counselors and resources to help plan for care costs.

The monthly rate covers room and board, three home-cooked meals daily, medication management, personal care assistance, housekeeping, laundry, activities, and 24-hour caregiver support. We keep it simple — one predictable rate, no surprise add-ons.

Adult family homes are ideal when a loved one needs daily hands-on assistance but doesn't require hospital-level care. Signs include difficulty managing medications, personal hygiene, or mobility, or safety concerns living alone. We're happy to do a free assessment call to help you decide.

We regularly reassess each resident's care plan and can accommodate increasing needs in most cases, including dementia progression, mobility changes, and hospice. If a higher level of medical care becomes necessary, we'll help coordinate a smooth transition with compassion and advance notice.

It starts with a simple conversation. Call or email us to share your situation, and we'll schedule a tour and assessment at your convenience. From there, we'll review medical records, complete a care assessment, and walk you through paperwork — usually within a few days.
