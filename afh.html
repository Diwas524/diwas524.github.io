Common Questions

Answers to What Families Ask Most

We believe in total transparency. Here are the questions we hear most often, answered honestly.

How much does Peace Park cost?+
Monthly rates vary based on the level of care your loved one needs. We offer a transparent pricing structure with no hidden fees. Most families find our rates comparable to assisted living, but with far more personalized attention. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your situation.
Does Peace Park accept Medicaid or Medicare?+
We accept private pay and work with families to explore all available funding options including long-term care insurance. Medicaid eligibility through Washington State's ALTSA program may apply, we're happy to guide you through the process and connect you with the right resources.
What's included in the monthly rate?+
Your monthly rate includes room and board, three home-cooked meals and snacks daily, all personal care assistance, medication management, laundry, housekeeping, scheduled activities, and 24/7 caregiver support. There are no surprise add-ons for standard care services.
How do I know if my loved one is ready for an AFH?+
Signs that it may be time include increasing difficulty with daily activities, safety concerns at home, loneliness or isolation, memory-related challenges, or caregiver burnout in the family. We offer free consultations to help you assess where your loved one is and what level of care makes the most sense.
What happens if my loved one's needs increase?+
We regularly review and update each resident's care plan as their needs evolve. Our licensed staff are trained to handle a wide range of care levels, including advanced memory care and hospice support. We partner with local healthcare providers to ensure continuity and always communicate proactively with families.
How do I start the admission process?+
It starts with a simple conversation. Call us or fill out the contact form and we'll schedule a free, no-pressure tour. We'll walk you through the home, answer every question, and if it feels like the right fit, we'll guide you through the assessment and move-in process step by step.
